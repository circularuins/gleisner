# Idea 004: Emoji & Stamp Reactions

**Status:** Raw idea
**Date:** 2026-03-16

## Summary

Timeline reactions go beyond simple likes — fans can react with emoji and stamps. Users can unlock additional emoji/stamp packs through a paid subscription or one-time purchase.

## Notes

- Default set of free emoji reactions available to all users
- Paid expansion packs add more expressive stamps and custom emoji
- Revenue model: user-side monetization (fans pay to enhance their own expression, not to unlock content)
- Stamps/emoji could be themed (music, art, seasonal, artist-collab packs)
- Reaction counts (by type) are visible on timeline items and affect visual appearance
- Related: Idea 003 (Fan Mode is the primary context for reactions)
- Related: ADR 005 (open-core model — reaction packs are a premium convenience feature)

## Impact on Timeline Visuals

- Multiple reaction types contribute to the item's visual "energy" (size, glow, color shifts)
- Different reaction types could influence the visual differently (e.g., fire emoji → warmer glow, heart → bloom effect)
- Total engagement (views + reactions + comments) collectively shapes how an item appears on the timeline
