# Idea 005: Synapse Timeline — Neural Network Metaphor

**Status:** Raw idea
**Date:** 2026-03-16

## Summary

The timeline is visualized as a creative neural network. Each content item is a neuron, and synaptic connections between items represent temporal and thematic relationships. The entire timeline becomes a living map of the artist's creative brain.

## Concept

- **Neurons** — Content items are displayed as organic, rounded shapes (not rigid rectangles). Each track could have its own characteristic shape or organic variation.
- **Synapses** — Curved lines connect related items:
  - **Temporal connections** (same track, nearby in time): strong, glowing lines in the track's color
  - **Cross-track connections** (thematic links, e.g., a composition inspired by a jam session): thinner, subtler lines bridging tracks
  - **Engagement-amplified connections**: popular items radiate more/brighter connections
- **The whole picture** — Zoomed out, the timeline resembles a brain's neural network, with dense clusters of activity and quieter periods

## Why This Works

- Aligns with Egan's "Diaspora" themes (consciousness, digital identity)
- Makes the "organic, living energy" goal concrete and coherent
- Gives the timeline a unique visual identity that no other platform has
- The metaphor is intuitive: creativity IS neural connections

## Shape Customization

- Artists could choose the shape character for each track (rounded, blobby, crystalline, etc.)
- Some randomness in each item's exact shape for organic variation
- Could be a customization feature (free: default shapes, paid: more shape packs?)
